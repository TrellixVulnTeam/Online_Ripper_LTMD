Welcome to Song Ripper!


Simply plug in a valid YouTube song or a public playlist link below and click Submit or enter to initiate the process. Before downloading the songs, you will be able to manipulate the downloaded mp3 files' metadata to your liking. When you have filled out the form for a file, click the Download button to receive a prompt to save the mp3 locally.

If applicable, move to the next song by clicking the Next button. When doing so, Song Ripper will delete the previous file on the server, meaning you will not be able to redownload it unless you process the song through Song Ripper as a separate link or through a new playlist. Please note, the Album field is how Song Ripper will be able to retrieve the album cover through Spotify's open API. By taking advantage of the Find Albums search feature, simply providing an artist name will call Song Ripper to retrieve a list of albums from Spotify, which can then be copied into the Album field. By making sure your spelling is correct, Song Ripper will be able to locate the album by your specified input. Song Ripper's features really shine when using YouTube playlists. By providing playlists' links, whenever new videos have been added, Song Ripper will remember previously downloaded songs by Song Ripper to prevent any redownloading. Song Ripper also allows for multiple playlists to be used and be remembered!
